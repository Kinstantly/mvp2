The Idea

Connecting parents to family services in more convenient ways

Kinstantly is based on a simple idea: Parents want an easier way to connect with the huge range of services they need — from preconception through high school graduation and beyond.

They want a better way to find a highly rated lactation consultant who makes house calls, a bilingual day care that has an opening for a toddler who isn’t yet potty-trained, an ADHD coach who’s great with teen boys and has Saturday hours. And they want to self-schedule on any device that’s handy: phone, tablet, or laptop. (No more phone and email tag!)

Providers, in turn, want someone else to handle the marketing and scheduling and payments, so they can concentrate on doing the work they love: helping families. They’re tired of losing money when they have no-shows or unfilled holes in their schedule. And they want an easier way to create a great web and mobile presence to attract new customers without spending a fortune to hire someone or taking forever to do it themselves.

That’s why we’re building Kinstantly. After talking to hundreds of parents and providers over the past year, we’ve zeroed in on some great solutions. We’re just getting started. But in the weeks and months ahead, we’ll be working to connect you all, dot by dot, in ways we think you’ll love.
