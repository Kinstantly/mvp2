About

Hours

Tues-Thurs: 3:45 pm to 8:15 pm

Availability/service area

Students can come to my studio in Emeryville or work with me by video anywhere.

Ages/stages

Ages 9 to adult, beginners to experienced players. I especially like working with advanced young students and adults, but I also excel at and enjoy helping beginners start off at a high level with good habits.
